About

Izzy Barkocy is a college freshman majoring in Game Design and Graphic Design and graduating in 2027. She has experience using multiple platforms, including Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Procreate. She makes digital illustrations and occasionally dabbles with Blender on the side. When she's not drawing, she enjoys ice skating, video games, Taco Bell, and spending time with her cat, Opal. Izzy currently lives in the St. Louis area while attending Lindenwood University.
